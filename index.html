Hello! My name is Roney Lacerda and I have been Digital Designer since 2002. If you need to just call me ✉

Data, User Experience & Visual Design

I've always been a cross-disciplinary designer - or what they call a "T-Shaped" profile - so I study constantly to be skilled in UX (research, wireframing), Design (visual, interaction) and Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, JS). I really enjoy to keep up with all the creative ecosystem.

I truly believe that the term design is strong enough to translate every step of a creative process, from research to the delivery of a project. I may seem a bit orthodox, but many of the fancy job titles we see today are the result of a dangerous fragmentation or desperate professionals to stand out in some way.

I am also interested in ... (hobbies and studies)

Data Visualization, Data UX, Statistics, Philosophy, and Literature — I maintain a repository and an account in Codepen with my studies, there you find some experiments with Sass, Javascript, Gulp (soon Vue.js and D3.js) and others.
