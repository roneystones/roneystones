Hello! My name is Roney Lacerda and I've been Digital Designer since 2002. If you need to just call me ✉

Data, User Experience & Visual Design

I've always been a cross-disciplinary designer (or what they call a T-Shaped profile). I constantly study to maintain my skills in UX (research, wireframing), Design (visual, interaction), Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, JS) and I really enjoy keeping up with the creative ecosystem.

I truly believe that the term 'design' is strong enough to translate every step of a creative process, from research to the delivery of a project. I may seem a little orthodox, but I believe that many of the fancy job titles that we see today are the result of dangerous fragmentation or desperate professionals to stand out in some way.

I am also interested in ... (hobbies and studies)

Data Visualization, Data UX, Statistics, Philosophy, and Literature — I maintain a repository and an account in Codepen with my studies, there you find some experiments with Sass, Javascript, Gulp (soon Vue.js and D3.js) and others.
