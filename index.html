In 2006 I graduated in Design Digital at the Centro Universitário Íbero-americano. Then I studied Information Architecture at the Instituto Faber-Ludens and since then I have completed several online courses among the disciplines of UX, Design and Front-end.

I am also interested in Data Visualization, Data UX, Statistics, Philosophy, and Literature — I maintain a repository and an account in Codepen with my studies. There you can find some experiments with Sass, Javascript, Gulp (soon Vue.js and D3.js) and others.

I truly believe that the term 'design' is strong enough to translate every step of a creative process, from research to the delivery of a project. I may seem a little orthodox, but I think that many of the fancy job titles that we see today are the result of dangerous fragmentation or desperate professionals to stand out in some way.
