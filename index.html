Hello! My name is Roney Lacerda and I've been Digital Designer since 2002. if you need anything, call me ✉

I've always been a cross-disciplinary designer, so I work to maintain my skills in User Experience, Visual Design and Front-End Development. Yes, I really enjoy keeping up with the creative ecosystem.

I am also interested in Data Visualization, Data UX, Statistics, Philosophy, and Literature — I maintain a repository and an account in Codepen with my studies. There you can find some experiments with Sass, Javascript, Gulp (soon Vue.js and D3.js) and others.
